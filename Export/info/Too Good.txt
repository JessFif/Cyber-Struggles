A fairly simple phishing style is one where simply what they offer sounds incredible.

When reading emails like this, take a second and instead of thinking how good it might be for you, think about how possible that not only does that deal exist, but also how likely it is to be shared if it did exist. Additionally, if it requires you putting something forwards first then that typically means they plan on simply taking the small thing you offer whilst your guard is down.

Typically, a quick search or just taking a second to think it through, can validate/invalidate the claims that were made, and will lead to you being safer.
