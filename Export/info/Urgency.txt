One of the most common phishing styles is by pretending there is a fast reaction needed, often either something negative happening if you don't respond, saying that your account has been compromised, or something being offered for a very short limited time.

It's rare that something super important will be sent to you with such a short time frame, however this is often forgot in the heat of the moment. 

By getting into the practice of pausing instead of rushing when seeing phrases that try to push you forwards, you can greatly increase the chance of spotting these scams.
